Katherine Parr: 1512 - 1548


Childhood and Early Life: 1512 - 1529

Birth: July or August 1512 (exact date unknown) in Blackfriars, a district in London.

Parents: Sir Thomas Parr and Maud Green.

Childhood: She grew up in Blackfriars, in her parents townhouse. As for her education, she was taught and was fluent in French, Italian and Latin.
